Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of experience do I need to join the courses?

None! Our beginner-friendly approach ensures that you can start without any prior knowledge. We'll guide you step by step.

Are the courses self-paced or live?

Most of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn anytime. However, we also offer occasional live workshops and events.

Will I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, every course includes a digital certificate you can showcase on your CV or LinkedIn profile.

Do I need to install any software?

>We recommend some free tools depending on the course, such as Visual Studio Code, Unity, or Kali Linux. We'll help you set everything up.

How much does a course cost?

Our standard courses range from $199 to $499 depending on the course type and duration. You can find detailed pricing on each course page.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes! We offer early bird discounts, student pricing, and occasional promotions. Be sure to subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured with encryption.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

Yes. You can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase if the course has not been started. After that, partial refunds may be considered under certain circumstances.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Absolutely. Once you complete the course and pass the final project or assessment, you’ll receive a personalized certificate you can add to your CV or LinkedIn.

Can I access the course content anytime?

Yes. All our courses are available 24/7, so you can study whenever it suits you best — at your own pace.
